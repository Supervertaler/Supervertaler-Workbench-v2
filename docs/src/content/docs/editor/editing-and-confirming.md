## Editing a segment

Click on a cell in the **Target** column to start editing. Type your translation and press `Enter` to confirm and move to the next segment.

When you edit an empty segment, its status changes from **New** to **Draft** automatically.

## Confirming a segment

After editing, you can confirm a segment to mark it as reviewed:

- Press `Ctrl+Enter` to confirm and move to the next segment
- The segment's status changes to **Confirmed** (green dot)

## Auto-propagation

When enabled, confirming a translation automatically copies it to all other segments in the file with identical source text. This saves time when the same sentence appears multiple times in a document.

You can enable or disable auto-propagation in **Settings → General**.
